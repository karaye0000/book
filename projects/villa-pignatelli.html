Museum Illustrations
for Villa Pignatelli

Illustrations for the tactile panels of the Museo delle Carrozze in Naples.

A series of digital line illustrations created for the Museo delle Carrozze at Villa Pignatelli, as part of the broader Genius Loci accessibility project developed in collaboration with Icecubes.

The drawings were developed for twelve tactile panels with Braille, dedicated to the museum’s carriage collection. Based on the illustrations, tactile lines and Braille elements were later produced and printed as part of the final panels.

The style is based on fine line work, soft details and a slightly romantic atmosphere, so that the images could work both as a visual support for the tactile panels and as illustrations in their own right.
